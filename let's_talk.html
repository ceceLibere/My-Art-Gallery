About

PHOENIX ART GALLERY

Phoenix Art Gallery officially starts working in 2008 and for 10 years is a collective place for all art lovers.

Vision

VISION

Our vision is to be a regionally relevant art gallery focused on presenting selected artists on domestic and international art scene.

Work

WHAT WE DO

In our gallery you can find artworks of the authors with whom the gallery cooperates, as well as offer of artworks by old Macedonian authors - owned by private collections. We organize art events-exhebitions.
